You enter the contract date, closing date, property address, purchase price, financing type, and any special conditions. CloseTrack's AI generates a complete milestone calendar with specific dates, responsible parties, and priority levels for every step from contract to post-closing. It automatically accounts for standard contingency periods and flags any dates that fall on weekends.
CloseTrack is an AI intelligence layer — it handles the thinking, drafting, and analysis work. It doesn't replace document storage or e-signature platforms. Most users run CloseTrack alongside SkySlope, Dotloop, or similar tools: CloseTrack handles the AI tasks (timeline generation, communications, contract analysis, deadline alerts), while their existing platform handles document storage and signatures.
CloseTrack extracts key terms and flags potential concerns with high accuracy for standard residential contracts. It identifies financial terms, contingency deadlines, unusual clauses, and missing standard protections. It's designed as a first-pass review assistant — it catches things humans miss under deadline pressure, but it doesn't replace attorney review for complex transactions or unusual clauses.
